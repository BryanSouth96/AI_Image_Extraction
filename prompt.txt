This information can be presented differently in every image. Be sure to analyze the image and look for words, symbols, tables, or other formats of information that represent employee numbers and ownership percentage in corresponding countries.

Employee Scale (in 1, 1K, %): The scale used in the employee count. 

Contractors (Number or %): If it is specified that there a certain number of contractors employed, extract that number. 

Contractor Scale (in 1, 1K, %): The scale used in the contractors count.
